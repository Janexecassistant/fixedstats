MPVI Video Quality Scorecard

Use this when reviewing a technician's MPVI video. Score each behavior 1–5. Total updates automatically. Print clean copies for documented coaching. Designed for weekly video reviews — one per tech per week is the minimum to drive improvement.

How to use: Watch one video start to finish. Score each category. Use notes for specific coaching. Review with the tech within 48 hours — videos a week old don't coach well.

Video Quality Behaviors

1. Pre-Recording Setup Car at right height, area wiped, lighting positioned, background clean
2. Opening Structure Four-part opening: name, vehicle, finding, bridge — clear in first 10 seconds
3. Camera Technique Steady shot, landscape orientation, close enough to see, slow movements
4. Show, Don't Tell Hand on the finding, part fills frame, customer can clearly see what's being described
5. Lighting Subject well-lit, no dark voids, customer can see surface detail clearly
6. Audio Quality Clear narration, no background drama, no mumbling, speaking volume audible
7. Language & Tone Plain English, no jargon, calm not pushy, no fear language
8. Green / Yellow / Red Categorization Findings clearly categorized; greens included where applicable; appropriate urgency
9. Focus & Length 3 or fewer findings, 90 seconds or under, stays on subject throughout
10. Closing & Handoff Recap, names the advisor, calm confidence close, no pricing, notes to advisor sent
